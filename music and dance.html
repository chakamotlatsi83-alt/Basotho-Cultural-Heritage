Mohobelo

It is a traditional dance performed by males where age those not matter. The dance is performed holding same decorated stick and attire whereby dance is performed by raising up and downn of feet at the same time doing the same step.

This dance is performed by females where age is not a boundry.The dance is made by kneeling down and moving the upper body part.

Thomo

A gourd-resonated bow instrument made of mountain bamboo near the bottom end,and string made of horse hair. Traditionally played by ladies and women

Moropa

An instrument made of"morit'sonam" made of clay and wet sheep skin, as a result the skin tieded on the clay .The music instrument is played at weddings and normally played by cowboys.

Setolotolo

A mouth-resonated musical bow used by men for entertainment during travel, particularly in Lesotho.This music instrument is made up of cylindrical center section and two smaller arched pieces at the end. Additionalaly, it is played using body, string,resonator and plucking.

Lekolulo

A type of flute played primarily by herding boys as part of the cultural music of the basotho people.
